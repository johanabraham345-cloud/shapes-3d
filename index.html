Advanced Filaments & Resins

We stock engineering-grade materials suited for everything from rapid prototyping to end-use production parts.

PLA & PETG Pro

Technology: FDM

Perfect for rapid prototyping, concept models, and low-stress functional parts. Wide range of colors available.

  • Cost-effective
  • High dimensional accuracy

Tough & Clear Resin

Technology: SLA

Isotropic properties with incredibly smooth surface finishes. Ideal for injection mold prototypes and optical parts.

  • Smooth surface finish
  • High detail resolution

Nylon (PA12) & TPU

Technology: SLS

No support structures needed. Creates highly complex geometries with excellent mechanical strength and flexibility.

  • High strength
  • Complex geometries

Carbon Fiber PA

Technology: FDM / SLS

Carbon fiber reinforced nylon. Replaces machined aluminum parts with exceptionally high stiffness-to-weight ratio.

  • Metal-replacement
  • Extreme rigidity
